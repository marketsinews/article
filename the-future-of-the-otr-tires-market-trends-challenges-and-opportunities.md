<h3><strong>What Are OTR Tires?</strong></h3>
<p>OTR tires are specially designed for vehicles and machinery that operate off paved roads, such as bulldozers, excavators, loaders, and tractors. These tires are built to withstand extreme conditions, including rough terrains, heavy loads, and harsh environments. Given their critical role in ensuring the efficiency and safety of heavy equipment, OTR tires are indispensable in industries like mining, construction, and agriculture.</p>
