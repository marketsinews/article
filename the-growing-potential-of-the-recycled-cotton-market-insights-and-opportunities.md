<h3>What is Recycled Cotton?</h3>
<p>Recycled cotton is produced by reusing cotton fabric or cotton waste, which is then processed into new fibers. This process not only reduces the demand for virgin cotton but also minimizes textile waste, making it a sustainable alternative to conventional cotton production. Recycled cotton is commonly used in apparel, home textiles, and industrial applications.</p>
