<p><strong>What Are Liquid Agricultural Fumigants?</strong><br />Liquid agricultural fumigants are chemical solutions used to control pests, nematodes, fungi, and weeds in soil and stored crops. They are applied in liquid form, which then vaporizes to penetrate the soil or storage facilities, effectively eliminating harmful organisms. These fumigants are widely used in pre-planting and post-harvest stages to ensure healthy crop growth and prevent losses.</p>
