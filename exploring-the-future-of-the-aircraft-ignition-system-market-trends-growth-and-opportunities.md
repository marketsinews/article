<h3>What is an Aircraft Ignition System?</h3>
<p>An aircraft ignition system is responsible for igniting the fuel-air mixture in the engine's combustion chamber, enabling the aircraft to generate thrust. It is a vital component in both piston-engine aircraft and turbine-engine aircraft, ensuring reliable engine performance under various operating conditions. Modern ignition systems are designed to be highly durable, efficient, and capable of withstanding extreme temperatures and pressures.</p>
