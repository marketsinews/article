<h3><strong>What is the Photovoltaic (PV) Materials Market?</strong></h3>
<p>Photovoltaic materials are the building blocks of solar panels, enabling the conversion of sunlight into electricity. These materials include silicon, cadmium telluride (CdTe), copper indium gallium selenide (CIGS), and perovskite, among others. The market encompasses the production, distribution, and innovation of these materials, which are essential for manufacturing efficient and cost-effective solar panels.</p>
