<h3>What is Battery Swapping?</h3>
<p>Battery swapping is a process where an EV's depleted battery is replaced with a fully charged one at a dedicated swapping station. This eliminates the need for time-consuming charging sessions, offering a faster and more convenient alternative for EV users. The concept is particularly appealing for commercial fleets, such as taxis, delivery vehicles, and ride-sharing services, where downtime directly impacts profitability.</p>
