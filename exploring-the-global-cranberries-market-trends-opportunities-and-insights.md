<h3><strong>The Rising Popularity of Cranberries</strong></h3>
<p>Cranberries are no longer just a Thanksgiving staple. These vibrant red berries have carved a niche in the health and wellness industry, thanks to their rich nutrient profile. Packed with antioxidants, vitamins, and fiber, cranberries are celebrated for their ability to support urinary tract health, boost immunity, and promote heart health. As consumers become more health-conscious, the demand for cranberry-based products, including juices, supplements, and snacks, continues to soar.</p>
