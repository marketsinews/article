<h3><strong>What is Medical Device Package Validation?</strong></h3>
<p>Medical device package validation is the process of ensuring that the packaging of a medical device maintains its integrity and protects the device throughout its lifecycle. This includes during storage, transportation, and handling. The validation process involves rigorous testing to confirm that the packaging can withstand environmental factors such as temperature, humidity, and pressure, while also preventing contamination.</p>
<p>Package validation is not just a regulatory requirement; it&rsquo;s a critical step in ensuring patient safety and maintaining the quality of medical devices.</p>
