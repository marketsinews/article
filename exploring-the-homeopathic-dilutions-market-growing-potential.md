<h3>What Are Homeopathic Dilutions?</h3>
<p>Homeopathy is a system of medicine based on the principle of "like cures like." It uses highly diluted substances derived from plants, minerals, and animals to stimulate the body&rsquo;s natural healing processes. Homeopathic dilutions are prepared through a process of serial dilution and succussion (vigorous shaking), which is believed to enhance the therapeutic properties of the substance while minimizing toxicity.</p>
<p>These dilutions are available in various potencies and forms, including liquids, tablets, and ointments, making them versatile for treating a wide range of conditions, from chronic illnesses to acute ailments.</p>
