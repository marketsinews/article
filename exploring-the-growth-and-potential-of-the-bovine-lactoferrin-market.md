<h3>What is Bovine Lactoferrin?</h3>
<p>Bovine lactoferrin is a glycoprotein found in the milk of cows. It is known for its antimicrobial, anti-inflammatory, and immune-boosting properties. This multifunctional protein plays a crucial role in supporting overall health, making it a popular ingredient in dietary supplements, infant formula, pharmaceuticals, and even cosmetics.</p>
