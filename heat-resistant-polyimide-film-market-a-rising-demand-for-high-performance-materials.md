<p><strong>Key Drivers of Market Growth</strong></p>
<ol>
<li>
<p><strong>Electronics Industry Expansion</strong>: The miniaturization of electronic components and the need for materials that can withstand high temperatures have led to the increased adoption of heat-resistant polyimide films. These films offer excellent insulating properties, making them ideal for flexible printed circuits and insulating tapes.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Aerospace and Automotive Advancements</strong>: In aerospace applications, polyimide films are valued for their lightweight and durable nature, essential for high-performance components. The automotive sector, particularly with the rise of electric vehicles, utilizes these films for battery insulation and other high-temperature applications.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Renewable Energy Initiatives</strong>: The solar energy industry employs heat-resistant polyimide films in photovoltaic modules due to their ability to endure extreme temperatures, thereby enhancing the efficiency and lifespan of solar panels.</p>
