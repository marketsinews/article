<h3 data-pm-slice="1 1 []">Understanding Nanotechnology in Drug Delivery</h3>
<p>Nanotechnology in drug delivery involves the use of&nbsp;<strong>nanoparticles, nanocarriers, and nano-formulations</strong>&nbsp;to transport therapeutic agents to specific sites in the body. These advanced delivery systems improve drug solubility, extend circulation time, and enable controlled release, making treatments more effective while minimizing adverse effects.</p>
