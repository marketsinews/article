<p><strong>What Are Industrial Heat Shrink Labels?</strong><br />Industrial heat shrink labels are specialized packaging materials made from polymer films that shrink when heat is applied. These labels are widely used for product branding, information display, and tamper-evident packaging. They are highly durable, resistant to moisture and chemicals, and provide a 360-degree coverage, making them ideal for a variety of industrial applications.</p>
