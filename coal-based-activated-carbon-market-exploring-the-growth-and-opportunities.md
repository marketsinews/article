<h3>What is Coal-Based Activated Carbon?</h3>
<p>Coal-based activated carbon is a highly porous material derived from coal, primarily used for adsorption processes. Its unique structure allows it to trap impurities, contaminants, and pollutants, making it an essential component in industries like water treatment, air purification, food and beverage processing, and pharmaceuticals.</p>
