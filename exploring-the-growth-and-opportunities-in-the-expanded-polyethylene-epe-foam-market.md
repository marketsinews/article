<h3>What is Expanded Polyethylene (EPE) Foam?</h3>
<p>Expanded Polyethylene (EPE) foam is a lightweight, durable, and flexible material known for its excellent shock absorption, thermal insulation, and water resistance properties. It is widely used in protective packaging, insulation, and cushioning applications. EPE foam is non-toxic, recyclable, and cost-effective, making it a preferred choice for manufacturers across industries.</p>
