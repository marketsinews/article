<h3><strong>What is Industrial Filtration?</strong></h3>
<p>Industrial filtration refers to the process of removing contaminants, particulates, and impurities from liquids, gases, and air used in industrial operations. These filtration systems are essential for ensuring the quality of end products, protecting equipment from damage, and minimizing environmental impact. Common applications include water treatment, chemical processing, food and beverage production, pharmaceuticals, and oil and gas operations.</p>
