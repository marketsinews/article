<h3>What Are Pates?</h3>
<p>Pates are a type of savory spread or paste made from finely ground meat, seafood, vegetables, or legumes, often combined with herbs, spices, and other flavorings. Traditionally associated with French cuisine, pates have gained global popularity due to their rich flavors, versatility, and convenience. They are commonly enjoyed as appetizers, snacks, or even as part of gourmet meals.</p>
