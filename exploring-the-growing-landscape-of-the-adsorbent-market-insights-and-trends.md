<h3>What Are Adsorbents?</h3>
<p>Adsorbents are materials used to capture and remove impurities or contaminants from liquids or gases. They work by attracting and holding molecules on their surface, making them essential in purification and separation processes. Common types of adsorbents include activated carbon, silica gel, zeolites, and molecular sieves, each with unique properties suited for specific applications.</p>
