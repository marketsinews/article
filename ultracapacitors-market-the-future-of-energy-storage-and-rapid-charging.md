<h3><strong>What Are Ultracapacitors?</strong></h3>
<p>Ultracapacitors are energy storage devices that bridge the gap between traditional capacitors and batteries. Unlike batteries, which store energy chemically, ultracapacitors store energy electrostatically. This allows them to charge and discharge rapidly, making them ideal for applications that require quick bursts of power. They are also highly durable, with the ability to withstand millions of charge-discharge cycles without significant degradation.</p>
