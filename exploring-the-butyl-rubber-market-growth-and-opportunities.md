<h3>What is Butyl Rubber?</h3>
<p>Butyl rubber, also known as IIR (isobutylene isoprene rubber), is a synthetic rubber known for its excellent impermeability to gases, high resistance to heat, chemicals, and weathering, and superior vibration damping properties. These characteristics make it an ideal material for applications such as tire inner liners, pharmaceutical stoppers, adhesives, sealants, and industrial hoses.</p>
