<p><strong>What Are Foam Cutting Machines?</strong><br />Foam cutting machines are specialized equipment designed to cut, shape, and sculpt foam materials with precision. These machines are widely used across various industries to create foam products for applications such as insulation, cushioning, packaging, and decorative purposes. The machines utilize technologies like CNC (Computer Numerical Control), hot wire cutting, and laser cutting to achieve high levels of accuracy and efficiency.</p>
