<h3><strong>What Are Offshore Support Vessels?</strong></h3>
<p>Offshore support vessels are specialized ships designed to assist offshore oil and gas platforms, wind farms, and other offshore installations. These vessels perform a variety of tasks, including transportation of personnel and equipment, anchor handling, towing, supply delivery, and subsea construction support. The versatility of OSVs makes them indispensable in offshore operations.</p>

</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Expansion of Offshore Wind Energy</strong><br />The shift toward renewable energy sources, particularly offshore wind farms, is creating new opportunities for the OSV market. </li>
<li>
<p><strong>Technological Advancements</strong><br />Innovations in vessel design, automation, and digitalization are transforming the OSV market. <li>
