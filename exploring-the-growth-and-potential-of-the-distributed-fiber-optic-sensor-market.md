<h3>What Are Distributed Fiber Optic Sensors?</h3>
<p>Distributed fiber optic sensors are advanced sensing systems that use optical fibers to detect and measure changes in temperature, strain, vibration, and other parameters along the entire length of the fiber. Unlike traditional sensors, which measure at specific points, DFOS provides continuous monitoring over long distances, making them ideal for large-scale infrastructure and industrial applications.</p>
