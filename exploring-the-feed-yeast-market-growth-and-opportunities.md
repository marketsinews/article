<h3>What is Feed Yeast?</h3>
<p>Feed yeast refers to yeast-based products used as nutritional supplements in animal feed. These products are derived from Saccharomyces cerevisiae and other yeast strains, offering a rich source of proteins, vitamins, minerals, and essential amino acids. Feed yeast is widely used in livestock, poultry, aquaculture, and pet food to enhance animal health, improve feed efficiency, and boost overall productivity.</p>
