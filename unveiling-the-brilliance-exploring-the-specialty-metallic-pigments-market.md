<h3><strong>What Are Specialty Metallic Pigments?</strong></h3>
<p>Specialty metallic pigments are finely ground particles of metals or metal oxides that provide a metallic or pearlescent effect when incorporated into various materials. Common metals used include aluminum, copper, zinc, and stainless steel, while advanced pigments may also incorporate mica, titanium dioxide, or other compounds to create unique visual effects. These pigments are valued for their ability to enhance aesthetics, improve durability, and offer functional benefits like UV resistance and corrosion protection.</p>
