<h3>What is Cellular Concrete?</h3>
<p>Cellular concrete, also known as aerated concrete, is a lightweight construction material made by incorporating air bubbles into the concrete mix. This results in a material that is both strong and easy to handle, making it an attractive alternative to traditional concrete.</p>
