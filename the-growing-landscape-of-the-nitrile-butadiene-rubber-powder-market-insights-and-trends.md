<h2>What is Nitrile Butadiene Rubber Powder?</h2>
<p>Nitrile Butadiene Rubber (NBR) is a synthetic rubber copolymer of acrylonitrile and butadiene. Known for its excellent resistance to oils, fuels, and chemicals, NBR is widely used in industries such as automotive, aerospace, construction, and healthcare. When processed into powder form, NBR becomes even more versatile, enabling its use in applications like adhesives, coatings, sealants, and molded products.</p>
