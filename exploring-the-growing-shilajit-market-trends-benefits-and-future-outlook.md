<h3>What is Shilajit?</h3>
<p>Shilajit is a sticky, tar-like substance found primarily in the rocky mountains of the Himalayas, Altai, Caucasus, and other mountain ranges. Formed over centuries by the gradual decomposition of plant matter and minerals, it is rich in fulvic acid, humic acid, and over 84 trace minerals. Traditionally used in Ayurvedic medicine, Shilajit is revered for its rejuvenating, anti-aging, and energy-boosting properties.</p>
