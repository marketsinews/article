<h3><strong>What is Tecar Therapy?</strong></h3>
<p>Tecar Therapy, short for Capacitive and Resistive Energy Transfer Therapy, is a cutting-edge treatment that uses radiofrequency energy to stimulate tissue repair, reduce pain, and improve circulation. It is widely used in physiotherapy, sports medicine, and rehabilitation to treat musculoskeletal conditions, injuries, and chronic pain. The therapy works by delivering controlled energy to deep tissues, promoting cellular regeneration and accelerating the healing process.</p>
