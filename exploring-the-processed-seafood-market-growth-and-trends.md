<h3>What is the Processed Seafood Market?</h3>
<p>Processed seafood refers to fish and other marine products that have undergone various forms of preservation, packaging, or preparation to extend shelf life, enhance flavor, or improve convenience. This includes products like canned tuna, frozen shrimp, smoked salmon, fish fillets, and ready-to-eat seafood meals. The processing methods can range from freezing and canning to smoking and drying, catering to diverse consumer preferences and culinary traditions.</p>
