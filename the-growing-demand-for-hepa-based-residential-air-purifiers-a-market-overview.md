<h3><strong>Understanding HEPA Filters</strong></h3>
<p>HEPA filters are widely regarded as the gold standard in air purification technology. These filters are capable of trapping 99.97% of airborne particles as small as 0.3 microns, including dust, pollen, pet dander, mold spores, and even some bacteria and viruses. Their efficiency and reliability make them a popular choice for residential air purifiers, particularly for individuals with allergies, asthma, or other respiratory conditions.</p>
