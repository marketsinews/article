<h3><strong>What is Chemical Protective Clothing?</strong></h3>
<p>Chemical protective clothing (CPC) refers to specialized garments designed to shield workers from exposure to hazardous chemicals, toxins, and other dangerous substances. These garments are engineered to resist penetration, permeation, and degradation from chemicals, ensuring the safety and well-being of workers in high-risk environments.</p>
<p>From coveralls and aprons to gloves, boots, and full-body suits, chemical protective clothing comes in various forms, each tailored to specific industrial needs. The materials used&mdash;such as neoprene, PVC, and Tyvek&mdash;are carefully selected based on their resistance to different types of chemicals.</p>
