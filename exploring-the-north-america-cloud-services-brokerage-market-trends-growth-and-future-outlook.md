<h3><strong>What is Cloud Services Brokerage?</strong></h3>
<p>Cloud Services Brokerage (CSB) refers to intermediaries that help organizations select, integrate, and manage cloud services from multiple providers. As businesses adopt multi-cloud and hybrid cloud strategies, the need for effective brokerage services has grown. CSBs act as a single point of contact, offering services such as cloud aggregation, integration, customization, and governance to ensure seamless cloud operations.</p>

<ol>
<li>

</li>
